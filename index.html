Who

We Are

Aher Export is one of the leading fresh fruit, vegetable export company from India. our main activities of exporting of Grapes, Pomegranate, Onion, Pineapple, Capsicum, Orange, Lemon and Fresh Vegetables. Our products is directly picked from the farmers who have long experience of cultivating vegetables and fruits. Geographically We are Located at Nashik (India) which is the best place of production of Grapes, Onions, Pomegranate and Vegetables. Our primary aim is to provide quality products, maintainable with hygiene and cleanliness.
